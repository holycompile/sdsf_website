Introduction

The School of Data Science & Forecasting (SDSF), Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (DAVV), is a pioneering institute dedicated to education and research in data science, statistical modelling, and forecasting. SDSF offers interdisciplinary programs combining theory and practical skills ‚Äî including M.Tech, dual-degree, and executive courses focused on data analysis, machine learning, AI, and applied forecasting.

SDSF's mission is to train industry-ready professionals and research leaders through hands-on projects, industry collaborations, and a curriculum that blends mathematics, computer science, and domain knowledge. Our faculty are active researchers with collaborations across academia and industry, and our graduates work across sectors such as finance, healthcare, climatology, and government forecasting.

To learn more about our programs, research groups, and collaborations, explore the Vision & Mission or contact the department directly.
